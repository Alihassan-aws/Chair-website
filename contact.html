Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all our furniture. Items must be in original condition with all packaging materials. Return shipping costs may apply.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) and white glove delivery are also available for an additional fee.

Do you offer assembly services?

Yes! We offer professional assembly services for an additional fee. Our trained technicians will assemble your furniture and remove all packaging.

What materials do you use?

We use high-quality, sustainable materials including solid wood, premium fabrics, and eco-friendly finishes. All materials meet strict quality and safety standards.

Do you offer customization?

Yes, we offer customization options for many of our products including fabric choices, colors, and dimensions. Contact us for more information about custom orders.

What warranty do you provide?

All our furniture comes with a 2-year warranty covering manufacturing defects and structural issues. Extended warranty options are available for purchase.
