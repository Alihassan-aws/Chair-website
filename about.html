About Telford

Crafting modern furniture with passion and precision

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Telford emerged from a simple vision: to create furniture that seamlessly blends modern aesthetics with uncompromising comfort. What started as a small workshop has grown into a trusted name in contemporary furniture design.

Our journey began when our founders, passionate about design and craftsmanship, noticed a gap in the market for affordable yet high-quality modern furniture. They set out to create pieces that would not only look stunning in any space but also provide the comfort and durability that modern living demands.

Today, we continue to honor that original vision while pushing the boundaries of design innovation. Every piece we create tells a story of meticulous attention to detail, sustainable practices, and a deep understanding of what makes a house feel like home.

Our Mission

To design and create exceptional furniture that enhances the way people live, work, and connect in their spaces. We believe that great furniture should be accessible, sustainable, and built to last.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of modern furniture that inspires and transforms living spaces worldwide, while maintaining our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

Our Values

Quality First

We never compromise on quality. Every piece undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets our high standards for durability and comfort.

Sustainability

We're committed to sustainable practices, using eco-friendly materials and processes that minimize our environmental impact.

Innovation

We continuously explore new designs, materials, and technologies to create furniture that meets evolving lifestyle needs.

Customer Focus

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We listen, learn, and adapt to exceed their expectations.
