Frequently Asked Questions

Ordering & Payment

How do I place an order?

Placing an order is simple! Browse our collection, select your desired items, choose your preferred color and quantity, and click "Add to Cart." When you're ready to complete your purchase, click the cart icon in the top right corner and proceed to checkout. Follow the steps to enter your shipping and payment information to finalize your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. We also accept payments through PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay for your convenience.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, your payment security is our top priority. We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your personal and payment information. We do not store your credit card details on our servers. All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways that comply with the highest security standards.

Do you offer gift cards or certificates?

Yes, we offer digital gift cards that can be purchased in any amount between $25 and $500. Gift cards can be used for any purchase on our website and never expire. You can purchase a gift card through our website and have it delivered via email to your chosen recipient.

Shipping & Delivery

How long will it take to receive my order?

Processing time for orders is typically 1-3 business days. Once shipped, delivery times vary based on your location and the shipping method selected at checkout. Standard shipping usually takes 3-7 business days, while expedited options are available for faster delivery. You'll receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order is on its way.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries. International orders may be subject to customs fees, import duties, and taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient. Delivery times for international orders vary by destination but typically range from 7-21 business days after shipping. Please note that international returns are subject to additional shipping costs.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order has been shipped, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email containing your tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website and viewing your order history.

What if I'm not home when my order is delivered?

If you're not available to receive your delivery, the carrier will typically leave the package at your door or in a secure location. For larger items that require a signature, the carrier will leave a notice with instructions for rescheduling delivery or picking up the package from a nearby facility. You can also provide special delivery instructions during checkout if needed.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be in their original condition, unused, and in the original packaging to be eligible for a return. Some exclusions may apply. To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team with your order number and the items you'd like to return. You'll receive a return authorization number and instructions for sending your items back to us.

How do I exchange an item?

To exchange an item, please initiate a return for the original item following our standard return process. Once we receive the returned item, you can place a new order for the desired item. If you need assistance with sizing or product recommendations, our customer service team is happy to help. Please note that exchanges are subject to product availability.

How long does it take to process a return?

Once we receive your returned item, please allow 3-5 business days for processing. You'll receive an email notification once your return has been processed. Refunds will be issued to your original payment method and may take 5-10 business days to appear on your statement, depending on your bank or credit card company.

What items are not eligible for return?

For health and safety reasons, we cannot accept returns on items such as mattresses, bedding, and other personal comfort items that come into contact with the body. Custom or made-to-order items are also final sale. Additionally, items that show signs of use, are damaged, or are missing parts may be denied a refund. Please refer to the product description for specific return eligibility before making your purchase.

Product Information

How do I care for my furniture?

Each of our products comes with specific care instructions. As a general rule, we recommend using a soft, dry cloth to dust your furniture regularly. For spills, blot immediately with a clean, dry cloth. Avoid using harsh chemicals, abrasive cleaners, or furniture polish. For wood furniture, keep away from direct sunlight and heat sources to prevent fading and warping. Upholstered items should be vacuumed regularly and professionally cleaned as needed.

Do you offer assembly services?

Yes, we offer professional assembly services for an additional fee. During checkout, you'll have the option to add assembly to your order. Our trained professionals will assemble your furniture in your home and remove all packaging materials. If you prefer to assemble the furniture yourself, all necessary tools and instructions are included with your purchase.

What is your warranty policy?

We stand behind the quality of our products with a comprehensive warranty. Most items come with a 1-year limited warranty that covers manufacturing defects and workmanship issues. Some premium collections may have extended warranty coverage. The warranty does not cover normal wear and tear, misuse, or damage caused by accidents. Please refer to the warranty information included with your product for complete details.

Can I see samples of your materials and finishes?

Yes, we offer material and finish samples for most of our products. You can request samples through our website, and we'll mail them to you free of charge. Alternatively, you can visit one of our showrooms to see and feel the materials in person. Our showroom staff will be happy to assist you in selecting the perfect options for your space.

Didn't find the answer you were looking for?

Our customer support team is here to help. Contact us and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
