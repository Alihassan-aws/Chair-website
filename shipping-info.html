Shipping & Returns

Shipping Information

At Telford, we strive to process and ship all orders as quickly as possible. Please review our shipping policies below:

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-3 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.

Shipping Options & Delivery Time

  • Standard Shipping: 3-7 business days
  • Expedited Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days

Important Note:

Delivery times are estimates and commence from the date of shipping, rather than the date of order. Delivery might be delayed due to high order volume or other factors outside our control.

Shipping Rates

Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout. We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $100.

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information. You can track your order using the tracking number provided.

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we're here to help.

Return Policy

You have 30 calendar days to return an item from the date you received it. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. Your item must be in the original packaging.

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Contact our customer service team at returns@telford.com to request a return authorization.
  2. Once approved, you'll receive a return authorization number and instructions for returning your items.
  3. Package your return securely, including all original packaging and accessories.
  4. Ship your return using a trackable shipping method.

Refund Process

Once we receive your return, we will inspect it and notify you that we've received your returned item. We will immediately notify you on the status of your refund after inspecting the item.

If your return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original method of payment. You will receive the credit within 7-10 business days, depending on your card issuer's policies.

Important:

Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund. For exchanges, you will be responsible for the return shipping costs.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you received a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately at support@telford.com with your order number and photos of the damaged item. We will work with you to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

Need Further Assistance?

Our customer service team is here to help with any questions about shipping or returns. Contact us and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
